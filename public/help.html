Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a service? expand_more

Navigate to your dashboard and click on "Book Service" or "Schedule" from the menu. Fill in the required details about your service needs, select a date and time, and submit your request. You'll receive a confirmation once a professional is assigned.

How do I contact a staff member? expand_more

Use the chat feature in your dashboard by clicking the chat icon in the top-right corner. Enter the staff member's email address to start a conversation. All messages are saved and you'll receive notifications when they reply.

How do I update my profile information? expand_more

Go to the Profile section in your dashboard menu. Click on the profile icon in the top-right corner to view your current information. Profile editing features will be available soon.

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Payment information can be securely stored in your account for future bookings. Contact us for corporate billing options.

How do I reset my password? expand_more

Staff members can change their password in the Settings section of their dashboard. For clients and admins, password reset functionality will be available soon. In the meantime, contact support@servicepro.com for password assistance.

What are your service hours? expand_more

Our platform is available 24/7 for booking and managing services. Service professionals are available Monday through Saturday, 8AM-6PM local time. Emergency services are available for an additional fee.
